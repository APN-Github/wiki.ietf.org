# IETF Outreach

## Types of Outreach

EMODIR seeks to coordinate outreach activities for the IETF undertaken by individuals and organizations. This wiki page will consolidate materials to help future outreach activities. 

There are outreach activities can be divided based on:
- target audience 
- anticipated level of IETF participation 
	- knowledge sharing (how IETF works)
  - supporter
  - reviewer
  - coordination
  - full (sustained) participation 
- responsible organization

Some examples of outreach activities are listed in the table below
